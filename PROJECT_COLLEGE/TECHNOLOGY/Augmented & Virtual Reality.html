What is AR and VR?

Augmented Reality (AR) overlays digital elements onto the real world using smartphones or AR glasses.
Virtual Reality (VR) immerses users in fully digital environments via headsets.

Applications Across Industries

- Gaming & Entertainment: Creates deeply immersive experiences.
- Education: Virtual labs, historical reconstructions, and interactive learning.
- Retail: Virtual try-ons, product demos, and immersive showrooms.
- Healthcare: Virtual surgeries and mental health therapies.

Future Outlook

As hardware improves and 5G supports real-time rendering, AR/VR will become more mainstream and accessible. These tools may soon be integrated into everyday tasks, training programs, and workplace collaboration.

Conclusion

AR and VR are set to change how we learn, shop, work, and play. With the support of powerful networks and continued innovation, the boundary between digital and physical will continue to blur.
